Problem 4 - Rotate about phi and psi bounds
===========================================

$ ./a.out rotate 30 phi 64.63 < data/2GB1.pdb | ./a.out rotate 30 psi 44.65 > new-2GB1.pdb

To find the minimum distance I call the mindist subcommand, which calculates
the nearest non-adjacent atom.

Atoms are considered adjacent (and distances between them are ignored) if
  1) They are the same atom
  2) They are the C and N joining two peptides
  3) They are in the same peptide (exception: the carbonyl oxygen is only
     considered adjacent to the carbonyl carbon)

$ ./a.out mindist < arendsee-hw1/new-2GB1.pdb | awk '$5 >= 1'
